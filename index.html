How to Use This System

  1. Register a Patient: Go to the Patient Registration section to add a new patient's details to the system. Each patient gets a unique ID.
  2. Check-In for a Visit: Use the Check-In section. Select the registered patient and the reason for their visit to record their arrival time.
  3. Check-Out a Patient: In the Check-Out section, you will see a list of all currently checked-in patients. Add doctor notes and click "Check-Out" to complete their visit.
  4. View the Dashboard: The Dashboard provides a live summary of checked-in patients, total visits for the day, and recent activity.
